🎨 Sculptures & Art — Profit While Beautifying

  • Sculpture material comparison: Marble (beauty ×135%) > Sandstone (fast mining) > Wood (flammable, don't put indoors)
  • Quality price multiplier: Legendary (×10) > Masterwork (×5) > Excellent (×2) > Good (×1.5) > Normal (×1)
  • Small sculpture (1 tile) vs Grand sculpture (4 tiles): Grand sculptures are more cost-effective but harder to place
  • Place sculptures in dining room + recreation room = double mood bonus
  • A pawn with Artistic ≥ 15 dedicated to sculpture = money printer
  • A legendary marble grand sculpture can sell for 2000+ silver

⚙️ Crafting Efficiency Tips

  • Crafting ≥ 8 to reliably make components. Below 8 wastes materials
  • Place tool cabinets next to workbenches (max 2 connected) = +6% work speed
  • Check the material info panel before crafting gear — different materials have different protection, durability, weight, and beauty
  • Add clothing bills as "Make until you have X" to always maintain stock
  • Smelter: melt down low-quality raider weapons into steel = recycling
  • Tailor's bench dusters: set "Quality at least Excellent" = auto-recycle low quality ones
  • Place drug lab on sterile tile for faster crafting speed
  • Don't set bills to "Do forever" — it will eat through all your materials
