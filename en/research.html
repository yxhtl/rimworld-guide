🔬 Tech Tree

📋 Research Order Overview (Standard Start)

For Crashlanded starts. Priority is already sorted for you — just follow the order:

TechOrderWhy Research It First
Batteries1stWithout batteries, power cuts out at night. Solar panels are useless during the day if you can't store the energy.
Solar Panels2ndReliable self-generated power. Frees you from depending on steam geyser locations.
Air Conditioning3rdFreezer = food storage. No freezer means your food won't last the first winter.
Stonecutting4thWooden buildings burn down in one spark. Stone walls are a permanent home.
Microelectronics5thUnlocks the advanced research bench and comms console — call traders and request reinforcements.
Precision Machining6thYou can craft components. Repair and build without waiting for a passing trade caravan.
Advanced Research7thUnlocks bionics and shipbuilding tech — you're officially in the late game.
Geothermal Power8th24/7 stable 3600W. One geothermal generator powers most of your base.
💡 Core Logic: Secure power first → then food → upgrade materials → finally rush high tech. Don't skip steps in this chain or you'll have to redo everything.

🔄 Research Differences by Start Type

Each start type has a completely different rhythm. Don't force the Crashlanded roadmap onto a Tribal run:

Start TypeRecommended Research StrategyKey Difference
Crashlanded Power → Freezer → Stonecutting → Microelectronics → Precision Machining Three colonists with basic tech. Follow the standard route safely.
Tribal Complex Clothing → Complex Furniture → Psychoid Brewing → Rush Electricity Research is painfully slow. Don't chase electricity early. Get cold-weather clothing and furniture for survival first. Brew psychoid tea (beer substitute) for mood. Only then touch electricity.
Rich Explorer Microelectronics → Advanced Research → Precision Machining Comes with some tech and resources already unlocked. Skip the basics and rush late-game for rapid equipment advantage.

⚡ Research Speed Tips

Same playtime, double the research output — it all comes down to the details:

  • Place the research bench on sterile tile. A clean environment boosts research speed; dirt and mess slow it down.
  • Connect a Multi-analyzer to the research bench for +10% research speed. Well worth building one.
  • Crucial Assign a dedicated researcher. Don't make them split time with construction or mining — focus is the real speed boost.
  • Pawns with Intellectual ≥ 8 research significantly faster. Anyone below 5 shouldn't touch a research bench — it's a waste of time.
  • If researching at night, place a lamp by the bench. Darkness gives -20% research speed — don't skip a simple lamp.
  • Late-game, build multiple research benches with multiple researchers for parallel progress — tech tree takes off.
  • Use Research Accelerator packets when you have them. No point saving them forever.
  • Before researching critical techs, reassign your highest-Intellectual pawn to full-time research for maximum efficiency.
