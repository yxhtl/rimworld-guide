🏠 Base Building

🏗️ Room Layout Golden Rules

Rooms in RimWorld aren't just random squares — each room type has a "purpose" check that affects work speed and mood.


Room TypeRecommended SizeKey FurniturePriority
Bedroom5×5 (25 tiles)Bed, nightstand, dresser, flower pot⭐ Better impression = +1~2 mood per level
Kitchen4×5Stove (against wall), light⭐ Must be clean — dirty kitchen = food poisoning
Freezer8×8 minimumDouble walls, double doors, cooler ×2⭐ Set to -9°C, prepare for heat waves & outages
Dining/Rec Room8×8 (combined)Tables, chairs, recreation items⭐ Combined saves space & materials, double bonus
Research Lab5×6Research bench, multi-analyzer (later)⭐ Keep clean for faster research speed
Workshop10×10Workbenches, tool cabinets against wall⭐ Tool cabinet covers two benches
Hospital6×6Medical bed, vitals monitor, sterile tile⭐⭐ Survival is non-negotiable

🧊 Freezer Design

The freezer is the heart of your base — no freezer means no food stockpile, and no food stockpile means you don't survive the winter.

  • Double walls: Build two layers of walls around the freezer for twice the insulation
  • Airlock: Add a 1×2 buffer room with two doors to minimize cold air loss when pawns enter
  • Cooler placement: Red side faces outdoors, blue side faces the freezer interior
  • Temp setting: Target -9°C. Add a second cooler if one isn't enough — summer heat waves demand it
  • Backup power: Place a dedicated battery connected only to coolers — gives you 12 hours of buffer during power outages
⚠️ Common mistake: Door "hold open" not set to false — pawns leave the door open while hauling, all the cold air escapes. Always check door settings!

⚡ Power Grid Planning

  • Distribute batteries: Don't cluster all batteries together — use switches to separate into groups. Zzzt events only fry what's connected.
  • Indoor power: Place batteries and generators indoors (roofed). Rain causes explosions.
  • Progression: Wind/Solar → Geothermal (mid-game) → Hydroponics + Sun Lamp (late-game)
  • Wiring: Use conduits for long-distance transmission; regular wires inside rooms are fine.

🧹 Cleanliness & Beauty

  • Flooring: Prioritize flooring in the kitchen and hospital (sterile tile is best). Other rooms can wait.
  • Dedicated cleaner: Mid-late game, assign one pawn cleaning priority 1 to keep the base spotless.
  • Outdoors: Lay flooring or concrete to prevent trees from growing into your base and to stop fire spread.
  • Floor material: Wood looks nice but is flammable — don't use it in large areas. Stone tiles are the safest.
