🔒 Privacy Policy

Straightforward, no beating around the bush

📌 In a Nutshell

This site does not actively collect any of your personal information. You don't need an account, you don't need to log in, and you don't need to fill in anything to read the guides. However — the hosting platform and third-party tools automatically collect some basic data, detailed below.

🖥️ Hosting Service: GitHub Pages

This site is hosted on GitHub Pages, with all page files stored on GitHub's servers. When you visit this site, GitHub automatically logs:

  • Your IP address
  • Access time and page paths
  • Browser type (User-Agent)

This data is managed by GitHub — we can't see it or modify it. See the GitHub Privacy Statement for details.

📊 Analytics: Microsoft Clarity

This site uses Microsoft Clarity for behavioral analytics, to understand which pages visitors view and what they click. Clarity collects:

  • Page view history (which pages you viewed and for how long)
  • Click and scroll behavior (which links you clicked, how far you scrolled)
  • Device info (screen size, browser type, operating system)

Clarity does not collect personally identifiable information (no IP logging, no keyboard input recording, no form content). See the Microsoft Privacy Statement for details.

📋 Feedback Form: Tencent Survey

This site collects feedback via Tencent Survey (wj.qq.com). When you submit feedback, Tencent Survey processes the data according to its own privacy policy — we only see the content you fill in.

The feedback form is optional — skipping it doesn't affect your ability to read the guides.

🍪 LocalStorage

This site uses your browser's localStorage to remember your theme preference (dark/light mode). This data:

  • Only exists in your own browser
  • Is never uploaded to any server
  • Disappears when you clear your browser cache

This site does not use any custom cookies. However, third-party services (see below) may use their own cookies.

📢 Advertising: Google AdSense

This site uses Google AdSense to display ads. Google uses cookies to serve interest-based ads. These cookies are controlled by Google, not by us.

  • Google uses the DoubleClick DART Cookie to show personalized ads based on your browsing history
  • You can opt out of personalized ads in Google Ads Settings
  • Or opt out of multiple ad network cookies at once via the Network Advertising Initiative

For details on cookies used by third-party vendors (including Google), see Google Advertising Technologies.

🔗 External Links

This site contains links to external websites (such as Steam, Ludeon Studios, Mod pages, etc.). Once you click these links, your privacy on those sites is governed by their respective privacy policies, not by this site.

⚖️ Disclaimer

  • This site's guides are based on RimWorld 1.4+ with all DLC. Future updates may make some content outdated.
  • RimWorld and all its DLC are copyright Ludeon Studios. This site is a fan-made community guide, unaffiliated with Ludeon Studios.
  • All mods mentioned on this site are copyright their respective authors. Recommendations reflect personal usage experience only.
