📦 Raising Management — Pen vs Free-Range

  • Pen animals (chickens/cows/alpacas): Mark a fenced area → put down feed or grow dandelions → cull periodically to control numbers
  • Hauling animals (dogs/wolves): Set allowed area covering base + freezer → they find their own food (keep them out of your fine meal stockpile!)
  • Combat animals (bears/panthers/megasloths): Restrict allowed area to base perimeter → they auto-engage when enemies come
  • Mark animal forbidden zones: Kitchen, hospital, dining room — animals shed fur and make a mess
  • Auto-slaughter: Set "auto-slaughter" in the animals tab — automatically culls when population exceeds the limit

⚠️ Common Mistakes & Pro Tips

  • Don't keep boomrats as pets — one stray bullet sets off a chain explosion
  • More animals = higher wealth = harder raids → don't hesitate to cull
  • Bonded animal dies = master gets -8 mood for a long time → don't send bonded animals to the front line
  • No grass in winter = herbivores starve → stockpile hay or grow dandelions indoors in advance
  • Failed taming attempt enrages the animal → save before taming high-level animals
  • Pregnancy, egg-laying, and milk production all depend on health and hunger → well-fed animals produce more
⚠️ Note: Animals can get sick too (plague/flu). Plague spreads through the pen if untreated. Stock animal medicine and quarantine sick animals.
